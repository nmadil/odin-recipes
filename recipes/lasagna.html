Easy Lasagna

Home

Description

Store the cooked lasagna in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to five days. To reheat, cover the leftovers with foil and bake at 350 degrees F for about half an hour, or until the lasagna is heated through and the sauce is bubbly. To freeze, wrap the lasagna in at least one layer of storage wrap and at least one layer of aluminum foil. Freeze for up to three months. Thaw the frozen lasagna in the refrigerator overnight, then follow the reheating instructions above.

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, sauce, cheese, and often meat or vegetables. It is typically baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna can be made with a variety of ingredients, allowing for endless variations and flavors. It is a popular comfort food that is enjoyed by many around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain the excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  7. In a large bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and salt until well combined.
  8. Spread a small amount of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Layer half of the noodles in the dish.
  10. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  11. Top with half of the meat sauce.
  12. Repeat the layers once more.
  13. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  14. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
