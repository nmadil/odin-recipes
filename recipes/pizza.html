Pizza is a delicious and versatile dish that originated in Italy. It typically consists of a round, flat base made from dough, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. Pizza is baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly. It is enjoyed by people of all ages around the world and can be customized with a wide range of ingredients to suit different tastes and preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, olive oil, salt, sugar, and the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
  6. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a round shape on a floured surface.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the surface of the dough.
  8. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your desired toppings.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve your delicious homemade pizza!
